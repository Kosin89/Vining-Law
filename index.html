WHAT WE DO

Our Areas of Practice

Vining Law successfully fights personal injury cases in any number of areas, however, our focus is in the following fields:

Car accidents

From investigating liability to dealing with insurance companies, we ensure you receive the compensation due to you.

Slip & fall

If you have suffered a damaging fall due to someone else’s negligence, we can help you win a settlement.

Work-related injuries

Gain compensation for medical expenses and lost income if you suffer an injury at work.

Catastrophic injuries

In the event of a life-changing injury, we will make sure that any liable parties pay you appropriate compensation.

Medical malpractice

If you have suffered harm as a result of medical negligence, we will help you win the compensation you deserve.

Defective products

Injury from defective products can result in you receiving sizable payments for your suffering.

Dangerous drugs

We win settlements for individuals who have suffered illness or injury from medications with poorly specified side effects.

Tractor trailor accidents

Whether you need help pursuing insurance companies or representation in court, we help victims of tractor trailer accidents gain payment and justice.

Motorcycle accidents

If the accident you suffer is someone else’s fault, we will ensure that you receive full compensation for your expenses and injuries.
